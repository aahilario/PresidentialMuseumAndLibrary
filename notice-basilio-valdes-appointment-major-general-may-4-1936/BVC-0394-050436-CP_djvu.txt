Pursuant to the provisions of Article VII, 
Section 11, paragraphs (3) and (4), of the Constitu- 
tion, ana Section 22, paragraph (b) , of Commonwealth 
Act No. 1, you are hereby appointed, ad interim, 
Major General of the Army of the Philippines. 

By virtue hereof, you may qualify and enter 
upon the performance of the office, furnishing my 
Secretary with a copy of your oath. 


Respectfully, 



President of the Philippines
