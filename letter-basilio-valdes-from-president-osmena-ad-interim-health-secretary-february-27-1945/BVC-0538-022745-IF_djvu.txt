Manila, February 27, 1945 

Maj . Gen. Basilio 2. Valdes 
Manila 

Sirs 

Pursuant to the provisions of Article VII, Section 

11, paragraph (4) of the Constitution, and of Section 66 
of the Revised Administrative Code, you are hereby ap- 
pointed, ad interim. Secretary of Health and Public 
Welfare. 

By virtue hereof, you may qualify and enter upon 
the performance of the duties of the office, furnishing 
the Commissioner of Civil Service with a copy of your oath. 

Respectfully, 



President of the Philippines
