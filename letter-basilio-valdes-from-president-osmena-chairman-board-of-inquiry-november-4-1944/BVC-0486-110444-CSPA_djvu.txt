Tacloban, Leyte 
November 4> 1944 


Sir: 

Pursuant to the provisions of Executive Order No. 23 
dated November 2 , 1944> and in addition to your present 
duties, you are hereby appointed Chairman of the Board of 
Inquiry to investigate charges of disloyalty to the Common- 
wealth of the Philippines and the United States in the province 
of Leyte, and consider the legal problems and matters of public 
policy involved therein. 

You will serve in the Board without additional compen- 
sation. 


Respectfully, 



-'SERGIO OSMENA 
President of the Philippines 


Major General Basilio J. Valdes 
Chief of Staff, Philippine Army 
Secretary of National Defense
